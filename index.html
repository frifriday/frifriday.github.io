About Me

I am a Researcher at NREL, Golden, CO. I received the B.S. degree from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2012, and the Ph.D. degree in Telecommunications under supervision of Prof. Lijun Chen from the University of Colorado at Boulder, CO, in 2018. I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at NREL from 2018.5 to 2019.4. My research aims to design distributed optimization and control algorithms for networked systems, particularly, distributed real-time frequency and voltage controls that achieve system-wide optimality and robustness for future power networks with large number of controllable loads and multi-energy systems. My research mainly employs techniques and tools from optimization, control theory, machine learning, and game theory.
